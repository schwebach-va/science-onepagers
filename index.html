This site is the companion to Everybody’s Science, Mr. Schwebach’s in-progress book on thinking with evidence — currently in draft.

This is a growing library of one-page readings written for advanced high school students working at an introductory-college level. New topics are added over the course of the year — check back often. Each page is a single, self-contained reading with key vocabulary and a few questions to check your understanding. Teachers should also see the new Teaching tab above for classroom-facing guides, protocols, and reference material.
